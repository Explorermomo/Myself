Shibing Mo (莫世冰)

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the School of Artificial Intelligence at Xidian University, advised by Prof. Jing Liu.

My current research interests include automated algorithm design, model post-training, agents, and AI for EDA. I am interested in building practical AI-driven methods that can improve design automation workflows and make algorithm development more efficient.

Email / Google Scholar

Current Work

  • Building an agent system for EDA netlist analysis and joint placement.
  • Developing model post-training and offline deployment methods for confidential real-world usage scenarios.
  • Exploring self-evolution mechanisms for agent systems.
